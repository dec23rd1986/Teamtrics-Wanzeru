Wanzeru:

Your AI Team Assistant

Features



Schedules Meetings:
No more ping pong emails to find the right time. Wanzeru schedules your meetings with the press of a button.

Attends Meetings:
Wanzeru facilitates every meeting to make sure no time is wasted, all members are communicating, and everyone has the chance to speak and contribute.

Takes Meeting Notes:
Wanzeru attends meetings and, with the power of a sophisticated algorithm, summarizes meeting minutes for participants to access and review at anytime.

Create New Tasks
Wanzeru helps you maintain project momentum and individual accountability by easily creating and assigning tasks to group members.

Manage Tasks
Seamless integration with Google Tasks allows Wanzeru to help you manage your tasks and quickly see upcoming deadlines.

Badges
Are you a great team member? Wanzeru will highlight your strength in meetings by providing you with badges so your team knows what you rock at!
